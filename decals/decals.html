Instructions

Easy application: Simple peel-and-stick installation with a strong adhesive backing that leaves no residue upon removal.

How to apply:

  1. Clean the surface where the sticker will be applied.
  2. Peel off the backing paper carefully.
  3. Align the sticker on the surface and press firmly.
  4. Smooth out any air bubbles with a credit card or similar tool.

Care Instructions:

  • For optimal longevity, avoid exposing the sticker to extreme temperatures or direct sunlight for prolonged periods.
  • If needed, clean gently with a damp cloth.
